Stardew Valley Mods

2021, Completed

Telephone Purchasing revolutionizes the player's interaction with the in-game phone item, enabling seamless in-game transactions by introducing a convenient purchasing system. This mod enhances the gameplay experience by granting players the ability to browse and acquire items directly through their in-game phone, bypassing the traditional reliance on scattered in-game stores.

CommunityCenterAnywhere revolutionizes the player's interaction with the in-game "Community Center" menu, enabling seamless insertion of items into bundles. Unlike the default functionality, which restricts players from placing items within the designated slots, this mod empowers users to effortlessly contribute items to their respective bundles.

Stingbot

2022, Completed

Stingbot is an innovative all-purpose Discord bot, offering comprehensive tracking of guild member statistics that are often overlooked. Leveraging the power of a JSON file for efficient data storage, Stingbot seamlessly interfaces with the discord.py API, facilitating seamless communication between Discord servers and local or private servers. Designed with meticulous attention to detail, Stingbot fills the void in tracking functionalities, delivering invaluable insights and enhancing the overall guild management experience.
